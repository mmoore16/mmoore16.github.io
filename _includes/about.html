I received my Ph.D. in Psychology from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign in 2018, and was a Beckman Institute Postdoctoral Fellow from 2018 to 2021. I now work as an Associated Health Fellow with the War Related Illness and Injury Study Center (WRIISC) at the Veterans Affairs Palo Alto Health Care System, and as a Postdoctoral Scholar with the Department of Psychiatry and Behavioral Sciences at Stanford University School of Medicine.
